✅ Location patterns

This is an obvious win.

Now all of these update the same fact:

"I'm from Lagos."

"I moved to Abuja."

"I now live in Abuja."

"I'm based in Abuja."


Combined with the upsert, the memory stays current instead of accumulating conflicting locations.

✅ Importance tiers

This is the improvement I was happiest to see.

Previously:

Recent 8 facts

could accidentally drop something like the user's name.

Now it's more like:

Critical facts
        ↓
Important facts
        ↓
Normal facts

That's much closer to how a person remembers someone.

⭐ One tiny refinement I'd make (not urgent)

Instead of hardcoding:

Tier 1
name
nickname
language

Tier 2
from
age

Tier 3
likes
dislikes
favorite

I'd eventually let each fact carry its own priority:

{
  key: "project",
  value: "GIFT-MD",
  priority: 2
}

That way, if you later introduce new fact types (birthday, profession, timezone, etc.), you won't need to modify the sorting logic—just assign a priority.

It's a small design improvement, not something I'd stop development for.
